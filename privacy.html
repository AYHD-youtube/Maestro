MAESTRO is a free website to play instruments. It is ad-free and does not use personal data for any marketing purpose.
You can safely use this website and create a user account. MAESTRO is easy to enjoy at home as well as at school.

1.Personal Data:


I collect the following personal data from incoming emails or user registration only: Name, email address and account password.
Your data and password is stored in secure, irreversible form.You can revoke access at any time.
I keep your personal data until you delete your user account. To delete your user account or to request a copy of your personal data on file, please email me.

2.Cookies


I do not use cookies for marketing. I use first-party cookies to:
  • store your login information
  • remember your language preferences
  • identify whether you are logged in or not
  • analyze the use of Musicca.
Third-party cookies are used when logging in using Facebook or Google.
Visit your browser settings to disable cookies. Please note that if you disable cookies, the above features will not work.

3.Children's Privacy:


Section 1 describes the personal data I collect and how it's used. Children's data is stored securely and isn't used for commercial purposes.
If parental consent is required, the user must enter their parent's email address, and the parent will receive an email regarding their child's registration.
Parents have full rights to review and delete their child's personal information. To delete your child's Musicca user account, please contact me to request deletion of the account and all associated information.
